standard 5.2: Demonstrate Brand and Marketing and Traffic Analysis

This section overviews the use of tracking, copyright usage, and marketing. You can analyze website traffic by using various apps or plug-ins that monitor incoming traffic (such as Google analytics). Using this data, you can then adjust your marketing plans to better suit the incoming audience, which could in turn generate more viewers. The Web is a competitive environment that allows people to start their own market and business from their home, making it a unique plays for growth. Optimization of your marketing audience can help retain and grow your audience in the end run. Copyright, Trademark, and licensing are always important to watch out for, because using someone else's work without permission can get you into legal trouble. If you don't have the talent or the time, try to find web materials that are labeled for reuse and modification.


standard 5.3: Understand the Relationship Between the Web and Social Media

Standard 5.3 focuses on the use of social media allows users to interact over the web. RSS/XML feeds allow a direct input of these social media sites into your own websites, giving your site a more vibrant and lively experience. Social media allows for quick and easy collaboration and the spread of information in a quick period of time. People use avatars (digital persona) to keep themselves anonymous on the social media sites as a way to keep their identity secure. Everyone uses social media differently, and various sites adapt and adjusts to the users needs.
