This section overveiws the use of tracking, copyright ussage, and marketing. Using this data, you can then adjust your marketing plans to better suit the incomming audience, which could inturn generate more veiwers. The Web is a competiative envirmonent that allows people to start their own market and buisness from their home, making it a unique plays for growth. Optimization of your marketing audience can help retain and grow your audience in the end run. Copyright, Trademark, and licensing are always important to watch out for, because using someone elses work without permission can get you into legal trouble. If you dont have the tallent or the time, try to find web materials that are labled for reuse and modification.

Standard 5.3 focuses on the use of social media allows users to interact over the web. Social media allows for quick and easy collaberation and the spread of information in a quick period of time. People use avatars (digital personas) to keep themselves annonymous on the social media sites as a way to keep their identity secure. Everyone uses social media differently, and various sites adapt and adjusts to the users needs.
